Facts

  • Saab cars have air conditioning routed to the glove box so that you can store your chocolate inside!
  • Saab invented heated seats in 1971 and air conditioned or ventilated seats in 1997.
  • In 1993, Saab introduced a feature commonly found in aircraft cockpits called "Night Panel". When pressed, this button would only illuminate the speedometer and warning lights, eliminating potential destractions from the dash.
  • In order to increase safety and improve design, Saab created a compact cupholder that is stored in the dash and flips out when pressed.
  • Similar to aircrafts, Saab cars had the ignition port between the driver's and passenger's seats. They claim this design is more ergonomic, and is easy to use do to being close to the gearshift and handbrake.
  • Saab was one of the most innovative brands, they pioneered turbocharged engines with good fuel economy, had many safety and quality-of-life features that made the driving experience much safer and smoother, and much more!
